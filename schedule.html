Sprint weekends

China, Miami, Canada, Great Britain, the Netherlands, and Singapore each add a Saturday sprint race and its own points.

NEW

Madrid joins the calendar

The Spanish Grand Prix moves to a new street circuit in Madrid, while Barcelona-Catalunya keeps its June date under its own name.

22→21

A shorter season

Bahrain and Saudi Arabia were removed from the calendar this year, trimming the schedule and reshaping the run to the finale.
